About Me

Hello, my name is Nicole Walsh.


I grew up in the suberbs of Los Angeles with my mom, dad, and three younger brothers. I attended high school at an all girls Catholic school. Then I studied at Berklee College of Music for my Bachelor's degree. Once I graduated, I attended Belmont University for my MBA. Now I'm in a coding certficate program at UCLA.

From 2017 - 2020 I worked at Universal Music Group. I started as an assistant for Candace Berry in the Sales & Distribution department. After two years as an assistant, I became a coordinator on the Spotify account to then a manager on the Apple Music account. Now I'm Director of digital accounts at Saban Music Group.
